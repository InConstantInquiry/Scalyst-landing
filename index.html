The Problem

Most growth efforts fail for the same reason.

Wrong Move
Spending on ads before fixing your sales process

You’re getting people to your door, but they’re not buying. More traffic just means more wasted money. The problem isn’t visibility — it’s what happens after someone reaches out.

Wrong Move
Changing prices when the real issue is demand

You raise or lower prices hoping revenue will shift. But if people aren’t finding you or understanding what you offer, no price point will fix that.

Wrong Move
Hiring before you have a repeatable process

Wrong Move

The Five Bottlenecks

You only have one real problem right now.

Every service business hits one of these five walls. The diagnostic figures out which one you’ve hit — so you stop guessing and start fixing the right thing.

$
Cash Flow

You don’t have enough money in the bank to keep operating, or clients are paying too slowly.

This looks like: You’re profitable on paper but constantly stressed about making payroll or covering next month’s bills.

Signal: Less than 2 months runway, or 45+ days to collect

%
Margin

You’re making revenue, but too much goes to delivery costs or overhead. Not enough stays as profit.

This looks like: You’re busy and booked, but your bank account doesn’t reflect it. Revenue goes up, profit doesn’t follow.

Signal: Gross margin below 40%, or net margin below 10%

■
Capacity

You’re maxed out. You can’t take on more work without quality slipping or burning out.

This looks like: You’re turning people away, working weekends, or cutting corners just to keep up.

Signal: Using more than 85% of delivery capacity

→
Conversion

You’re getting leads, but not enough become paying clients. Something in your sales process is leaking.

This looks like: People reach out, book calls, show interest — then disappear. You’re doing the work to get attention, but it’s not turning into revenue.

Signal: Closing fewer than 20% of leads

▲
Lead Volume

Everything else is working. You just need more people coming through the door.

This looks like: Your clients are happy, your margins are healthy, your process works — you just need more of it.

Signal: Conversion, margins, and capacity are all fine
